Amazing chicken recipe

2024/08/28

All you really need to make an amazing marinade is

  • 6 chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • Any Italian herb mix

Recipe

On cutting board

  1. Cut up chicken breasts with a butterfly filet
  2. Use fork to create holes in chicken breasts In mixing bowl
  3. Squeeze lemon
  4. Add enough olive oil to cover chicken
  5. Cut up garlic gloves
  6. cover surface of olive oil with herb mix
  7. mix everything

Wait >= 30 min. for marinage. I bake at 375 deg Fahrenheit for 35 min.

This recipe is absolutely delicious, amazing, yummy, awesome. You can even do homework while it bakes
